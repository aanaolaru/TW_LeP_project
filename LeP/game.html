    Welcome to the Quiz Game
    Put your knowledge to the test with this fun quiz!
    You will have to pass 3 levels in order to see the ranking and your position in it.

    Each level has a certain difficulty: easy, medium and hard .

    Consider the rules at the beginning of each level and read each question carefully choosing / writing the response that you feel is correct.

    For each level there are 10 questions. After submiting, you will receive a score that will be added to the final score.

    There is a specific time for each level which will appear permanently in the upper left corner so that you know all the time how many seconds you have left to solve.
